## Actividad 1

**Instrucciones:** Construir la tabla y graficar $r = 4$.

**Respuesta:** Considerando el par ordenado $(x, y)$ equivalente a $(r \cdot \cos\theta,\; r \cdot \sin\theta)$:

| $\theta$ | $r$ | $(r, \theta)$ | $(x, y)$ |
|----------|-----|---------------|----------|
| $0$ | 4 | $(4, 0)$ | $(4, 0)$ |
| $\frac{\pi}{4}$ | 4 | $\left(4, \frac{\pi}{4}\right)$ | $(2\sqrt{2}, 2\sqrt{2})$ |
| $\frac{\pi}{2}$ | 4 | $\left(4, \frac{\pi}{2}\right)$ | $(0, 4)$ |
| $\frac{3\pi}{4}$ | 4 | $\left(4, \frac{3\pi}{4}\right)$ | $(-\sqrt{2}, 2\sqrt{2})$ |
| $\pi$ | 4 | $(4, \pi)$ | $(-4, 0)$ |
| $\frac{5\pi}{4}$ | 4 | $\left(4, \frac{5\pi}{4}\right)$ | $(-2\sqrt{2}, -2\sqrt{2})$ |
| $\frac{3\pi}{2}$ | 4 | $\left(4, \frac{3\pi}{2}\right)$ | $(0, -4)$ |
| $\frac{7\pi}{4}$ | 4 | $\left(4, \frac{7\pi}{4}\right)$ | $(2\sqrt{2}, -2\sqrt{2})$ |
| $2\pi$ | 4 | $(4, 2\pi)$ | $(4, 0)$ |

| $\theta$ | $r$ | $(r, \theta)$ | $(x, y)$ |
