## Actividad 1

**Instrucciones:** Construir la tabla de valores para $x = t$ y $y = t^2$ usando $t = -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3$.

**Respuesta:**

| $t$ | $x$ | $y$ |
|-----|-----|-----|
| $-3$ | $-3$ | $(-3)^2 = 9$ |
| $-2$ | $-2$ | $(-2)^2 = 4$ |
| $-1$ | $-1$ | $(-1)^2 = 1$ |
| $0$ | $0$ | $0^2 = 0$ |
| $1$ | $1$ | $1^2 = 1$ |
| $2$ | $2$ | $2^2 = 4$ |
| $3$ | $3$ | $3^2 = 9$ |

## Actividad 2

**Instrucciones:** Determinar los puntos de la circunferencia $x = 4\cos(t)$ y $y = 4\sin(t)$ para $t = 0°, 90°, 180°, 270°$.

**Respuesta:**

| $t$ | $x$ | $y$ | $(x, y)$ |
|-----|-----|-----|----------|
| $0°$ | $4\cos(0°) = 4$ | $4\sin(0°) = 0$ | $(4, 0)$ |
| $90°$ | $4\cos(90°) = 0$ | $4\sin(90°) = 4$ | $(0, 4)$ |
| $180°$ | $4\cos(180°) = -4$ | $4\sin(180°) = 0$ | $(-4, 0)$ |
| $270°$ | $4\cos(270°) = 0$ | $4\sin(270°) = -4$ | $(0, -4)$ |

## Actividad 3

**Instrucciones:** Graficar la elipse con $x = 6\cos(t)$ y $y = 2\sin(t)$ utilizando al menos ocho valores de $t$.

**Respuesta:**

| $t$ | $x$ | $y$ | $(x, y)$ |
|-----|-----|-----|----------|
| $0$ | $6\cos(0) = 6$ | $2\sin(0) = 0$ | $(6, 0)$ |
| $\frac{\pi}{4}$ | $6\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right) \approx 4.24$ | $2\sin\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right) \approx 1.41$ | $(4.24, 1.41)$ |
| $\frac{\pi}{2}$ | $6\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right) = 0$ | $2\sin\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right) = 2$ | $(0, 2)$ |
| $\frac{3\pi}{4}$ | $6\cos\left(\frac{3\pi}{4}\right) \approx -4.24$ | $2\sin\left(\frac{3\pi}{4}\right) \approx 1.41$ | $(-4.24, 1.41)$ |
| $\pi$ | $6\cos(\pi) = -6$ | $2\sin(\pi) = 0$ | $(-6, 0)$ |
| $\frac{5\pi}{4}$ | $6\cos\left(\frac{5\pi}{4}\right) \approx -4.24$ | $2\sin\left(\frac{5\pi}{4}\right) \approx -1.41$ | $(-4.24, -1.41)$ |
| $\frac{3\pi}{2}$ | $6\cos\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = 0$ | $2\sin\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = -2$ | $(0, -2)$ |
| $\frac{7\pi}{4}$ | $6\cos\left(\frac{7\pi}{4}\right) \approx 4.24$ | $2\sin\left(\frac{7\pi}{4}\right) \approx -1.41$ | $(4.24, -1.41)$ |
